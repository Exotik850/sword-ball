# Sword Ball

Exciting arcade-style game where players control a physics based ball with a weapon attached to it. Swing the weapon to hit your targets and be the last player standing!

## Gameplay 

- Control a ball with realistic physics.
- Attach a sword to the ball and swing it to hit opponents.
- Die upon being flung out of the arena.
- Last player standing wins the round.
- Multiple arenas with different layouts and obstacles.
- Power-ups and special abilities to enhance gameplay.
